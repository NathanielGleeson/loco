About us

Your Total Solution for Locomotive Engines

The Locomotive Company has been selling large-bore diesel engine replacement parts for over 35 years and is an established international supplier.

Our locally owned business can supply you with new off-the-shelf items as well as reconditioned and RTO (running take out) parts.

We specialize in the procurement of parts that are hard-to-find, discontinued, or no longer produced by the original manufacturer. Our knowledgeable employees can perform worldwide on-site inspections and evaluations of your equipment. All new parts come with a warranty, and nearly all parts that leave our manufacturing facility come backed by a warranty. Export (wood) boxing is available upon request. The Locomotive Company is happy to provide quotes to any customers who need immediate attention.

Depending on your situation, good, used, or reconditioned locomotive engine parts can save you time and money. Utilizing our UTEX (unit exchange) program, you can shorten any turnaround and downtime on your equipment.

With our experience and vast resource library, The Locomotive Company can supply you with in-depth technical knowledge on locomotive engine parts. In many cases, we can provide you with original copies of technical, mechanical, operational, and parts manuals for most manufacturers.
